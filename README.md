# BreakerRobot
Raspberry Pi with OLED Adafruit 326 display. Making a standing robot


Pinout:
  Beeper:
    - 39 (Ground)
    - 40 (GPIO 21)
 Buttons:
    - 31 (GPIO 6)
    - 29 (GPIO 5)
    - 11 (GPIO 17)
    - 13 (GPIO 27)
    - 2 (5V power)
    
 SPI Display:
    - 1 (3V3 power)
    - 6 (Ground)
    - 23 (GPIO 11 (SPI0 SCLK))
    - 19 (GPIO 10 (SPI0 MOSI))
    - 24 (GPIO 8 (SPI0 CE0))
    - 22 (GPIO 25)
    - 18 (GPIO 24)
